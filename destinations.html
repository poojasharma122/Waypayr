Top Destinations in New Jersey

New Jersey is one of the most diverse travel states on the East Coast. You’ll find big-city buzz, quiet beach towns, forest trails, food scenes, cultural landmarks, and college towns — all within a couple of hours’ drive. Whether you're building a weekend escape, a longer trip, or planning day-by-day, this guide covers the top places to visit across the Garden State. Use this page as your starting point.
