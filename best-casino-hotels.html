Best Casino Hotels and Resorts in Atlantic City

Choosing the right place to stay is key — most travellers come for the full casino hotel experience, and Atlantic City offers some of the best on the coast. These resorts are packed with everything from gaming floors and rooftop pools to high-end restaurants and on-site shopping.

Borgata Hotel Casino & Spa

Located in the Marina District, Borgata is known for its spacious rooms, full-service spa, large casino floor, and lineup of live entertainment. It's one of the most complete stays in the city — ideal for couples, groups, or solo travellers.

Hard Rock Hotel & Casino Atlantic City

Right on the boardwalk, the Hard Rock offers direct beach access, a wide selection of dining, a large gaming area, and live music most nights of the week. It’s a great all-round choice for travellers who want energy and convenience.

Ocean Casino Resort

Modern and stylish with unmatched ocean views, Ocean Resort combines a high-end casino floor with rooftop pools, wellness spaces, and floor-to-ceiling windows in every room. One of the newest resorts in Atlantic City, and one of the most visually impressive.

Caesars Atlantic City

Classic in design and location, Caesars sits right in the middle of the boardwalk with easy access to the beach and The Playground shopping mall. Known for its wide range of food options and a large casino floor

Tropicana Atlantic City

Tropicana feels like a small city inside a resort. It has multiple towers, a broad mix of restaurants and bars, a massive casino, and The Quarter — an indoor complex with shops, nightlife, and dining. Great for long weekends or those who like options under one roof.
