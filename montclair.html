Montclair

Montclair offers a city feel in a leafy, laid-back setting. Known for its strong creative community, the town is packed with art galleries, small theatres, vintage shops, and indie bookstores. You’ll also find excellent restaurants, coffee shops, and wine bars scattered across its downtown districts.

Montclair sits at the edge of the Watchung Mountains, giving it natural elevation and plenty of nearby hiking trails. Events like film festivals, art walks, and food tastings happen throughout the year. It’s ideal for couples, culture lovers, or anyone looking to step away from the usual destinations.
